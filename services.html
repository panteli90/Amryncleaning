What We Offer

Commercial Cleaning Services

From daily office cleans to large-scale commercial contracts — we cover it all with a consistent, professional approach.

Service 01

Office Cleaning

A clean office is a productive office. We provide flexible office cleaning across all sizes of workspace — from small teams to multi-floor corporate offices. Our staff are trained, vetted, and understand the importance of working discreetly around your business hours.

Whether you need us daily before staff arrive, or a weekly deep clean on a Friday evening, we'll build a schedule around you.

  • Daily, weekly or bespoke scheduling
  • Desks, kitchens, toilets & common areas
  • Waste removal and consumable restocking
  • Floor maintenance — vacuuming, mopping, buffing
  • Window and glass cleaning (internal)
  • Disinfection and sanitisation protocols
  • Fully insured and DBS-checked staff

Service 02

Retail & Commercial Cleaning

High-footfall commercial spaces demand more — more frequency, more thoroughness, and cleaning that fits around trading hours. We service supermarkets, banks, shopping centres, and retail outlets with a practical, no-fuss approach.

Our teams work efficiently to ensure your space is clean and compliant without disrupting your customers or staff.

  • Supermarket and food retail cleaning
  • Bank and financial premises cleaning
  • Shopping centre and unit cleans
  • Floor scrubbing and polishing
  • High-touch surface sanitisation
  • Out-of-hours and early-morning scheduling
  • Emergency reactive cleaning available

Service 03

Commercial Building Site Cleans

Builder's cleans, sparkle cleans, and ongoing site maintenance — we work with developers, contractors, and property managers to get sites ready for occupation or inspection.

We understand construction site timelines. We're flexible, fast, and know how to work safely on active sites.

  • Builder's cleans post-construction
  • Sparkle cleans prior to handover
  • Dust and debris clearance
  • Window and frame cleaning
  • Communal area and stairwell cleans
  • COSHH-compliant chemical use
  • Safe site working — RAMS available on request

Service 04

Janitorial Contracts

For businesses that need a permanent cleaning presence, we provide dedicated janitorial contracts with assigned staff who become part of your team. They know your site, your standards, and your people.

This is the most cost-effective solution for larger premises or those with high-frequency cleaning needs throughout the working day.

  • Dedicated on-site cleaning operative
  • Flexible hours — full or part-time
  • Continuous daytime cleaning and maintenance
  • Consumables supply and management
  • Regular management check-ins and reporting
  • Cover during absence included
  • Fully employed, insured, and managed by Amryn
